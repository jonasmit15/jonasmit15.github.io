I am currently a PhD candidate in mathematics at the University of South Carolina. My doctoral advisor is Alex Duncan, and I intend to graduate in the spring of 2024.

My research is broadly in algebraic geometry, but I am particularly interested in birational geometry, Galois cohomology, Fano varieties, and Cremona groups (to name a few topics). Most recently, I have been investigating classes of automorphisms on Del Pezzo surfaces over general fields.

  • In the fall of 2022, I am teaching Math 111I (College Algebra).
  • Along with Pat Lank, I am co-organizing the Graduate Colloquium at UofSC for the 2022-2023 academic year.
  • I am also a participant of the AGCA Student Seminar and a member of the SCAGNT group at UofSC
